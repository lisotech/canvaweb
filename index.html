PACRA Limited Company Registration

Fee: K3,500

Requirements

Name Clearance

You suggest three names then after approved.

Directors Details

Minimum of two with the following info:

  • Full Names of Director
  • Email addresses
  • Contact Numbers
  • NRC Numbers
  • Date of Birth
  • Residential address
  • Name of a Road nearby
  • The date which the business will start operating
  • Financial year end
  • Front and Back Copies of NRC

Upon Completion

The PACRA Certificate is given immediately.

PACRA Business Registration

Fee: K1,200

Requirements

Name Clearance

You suggest three names which will be approved by PACRA.

Directors Details

Each director must provide:

  • Full Names
  • Email addresses
  • Contact Numbers
  • NRC Numbers
  • Date of Birth
  • Residential Address
  • Name of a Road Nearby
  • Date the Business Will Start Operating
  • Financial Year End
  • Front and Back Copies of NRC

Upon Completion

The PACRA Certificate and ZRA T-PIN Number Certificate are issued immediately upon completion of the registration process.
